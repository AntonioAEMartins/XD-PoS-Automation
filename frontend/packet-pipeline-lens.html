The reading lens.

One long byte stream and a draggable glass lens. Wherever the lens sits, a panel above explains that region in plain language. Best suited for a docs deep-dive or annotated chapter page where the reader is in slow-reading mode and has the room to run their finger along the wire.

Source · packet-transformation-explorations.html · Approach E

Lens is on POSTQUEUE
The opcode. "Hey box, I'm posting something to your queue." Always the very first nine bytes of any message of this kind.

Drag the glassy rectangle — it snaps to whatever zone of the wire it’s closest to. Arrow keys also work.
